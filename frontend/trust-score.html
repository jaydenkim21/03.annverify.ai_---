The Grade Scale

Scores are weighted across multiple verification dimensions and presented as a clear grade.

A
80 – 100
Verified

Strong evidence supports the claim. Multiple reliable sources agree.

B
60 – 79
Likely True

Generally supported with minor gaps or limited sourcing.

C
40 – 59
Uncertain

Mixed or inconclusive evidence. Interpret with caution.

D
0 – 39
False / Misleading

Evidence contradicts the claim or sources are unreliable.

What Affects Your Score

Source Credibility

Domain authority, editorial standards, and historical accuracy of cited sources all contribute to the score.

Evidence Consistency

How consistently the claim is supported across independent sources. Contradictory coverage lowers the score.

Recency & Context

Claims are evaluated against the most current available information. Outdated context is flagged in the report.
